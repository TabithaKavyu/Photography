About Me

I am a cool, calm and collected Photographer who is flexible and passionate about my job. A creative who is enthusiastic and not afraid of going all out to achieve client satisfaction. I am a very easy and profound guy who is able to dive into anything. I never stop learning. I spend my time studying and practising new skills and techniques. I am traversing the photography industry to capture the best. I am now a Real Estate and commercial photographer based in Nairobi.

I have had the opportunity to work for major brands like :

  • Palm Golding Properties Kenya
  • Gloria's Cake House
  • Irrihub Kenya
  • Raddison Blu
I am tirelessly working on capturing the exclusive character of people and property molded by the provoking use of light and composition
